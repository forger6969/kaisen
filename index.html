Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a Japanese anime and manga series about a high school student, Yuji Itadori, who gets involved in a secret world of cursed spirits after swallowing a powerful cursed object. To protect others, he joins a group of sorcerers trained to fight curses, led by the strongest sorcerer, Satoru Gojo. The series blends intense action, dark themes, and emotional storytelling.

Jujutsu Kaisen Heros

Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo is the most powerful jujutsu sorcerer and a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High. He’s known for his Six Eyes, blindfold, and unmatched cursed technique, Limitless.

Suguru Geto

Suguru Geto is a former jujutsu sorcerer and once Gojo’s best friend. He became a powerful curse user, believing only sorcerers should exist, and seeks to eliminate all non-sorcerers.

Yuji Itadori

Yuji Itadori is a kind-hearted high school student who becomes a jujutsu sorcerer after swallowing Sukuna’s finger. He fights curses to protect others and carry out meaningful deaths. Ask
